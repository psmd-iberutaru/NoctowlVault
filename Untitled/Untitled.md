

After years of fighting, the Algorians ultimately succumbed to the mighty fleet of the Demo. The last city burned to the ground as it bore witness to the insurmountable power that lay across the sea. 

But hope was not all lost. Out of the sacrifice of every man and woman who defended their nation, came survivors--including the young princess Anne, now last of the royal family.

What lies ahead for these survivors will not be easy, but they must survive and never lost hope...

Agloria lives on.
